= Gossamer — Governance, Legal & Risk
:toc:

This page is the *signpost* for how Gossamer is governed, licensed, and kept
honest about its own risk — a business-case summary pointing to the canonical
documents, not replacing them. Gossamer is a research-estate component (v0.3.x,
alpha, ~92% MVP, 192 integration tests), not a commercial product; this document
holds to that standard: evidence first, no aspirational claims.

== Licensing

Gossamer ships under **MPL-2.0** — file-level copyleft that keeps changes to
Gossamer's own files open while staying friendly to commercial integration.
Copyright (c) 2025-2026 Jonathan D.A. Jewell.

* Licence text and its Palimpsest-MPL exhibits are maintained centrally at
https://github.com/hyperpolymath/palimpsest-license[hyperpolymath/palimpsest-license].
The ethical-use and quantum-safe exhibits under `docs/legal/` are *non-binding*
guidance, not additional licence terms.
* Gossamer is built to be embedded. Its principal consumer,
https://github.com/hyperpolymath/idaptik[IDApTIK], is **AGPL-3.0-or-later**;
MPL-2.0's file-level scope composes with that stronger copyleft at the boundary,
so Gossamer can live inside an AGPL application without relicensing either side.
* Documentation (this file included) is CC-BY-SA-4.0. Tracked housekeeping:
Gossamer's own tree was reconciled to MPL-2.0 (394 files + `.ipkg`) in the
2026-06-02 sweep; residual AGPL-vs-MPL header drift in sibling repos is logged.

== Governance apparatus

Gossamer follows the **Rhodium Standard Repository (RSR)** template, adopted in
https://github.com/hyperpolymath/gossamer/blob/main/docs/decisions/0001-adopt-rsr-standard.adoc[ADR-0001].
RSR fixes repository shape (machine-readable state, security/governance files,
SHA-pinned workflows) so structure does not drift per-repo. Policy is enforced
by **contractiles** — a six-verb, machine-readable model
(https://github.com/hyperpolymath/gossamer/blob/main/.machine_readable/contractiles/INDEX.a2ml[INDEX.a2ml]):

[cols="1,4,2",options="header"]
|===
| Verb | Semantics | Authority
| `must`   | release-blocking invariants | blocking
| `trust`  | security + provenance + safe-hacking | blocking
| `bust`   | hard-stop / deprecated-path-reintroduction guards | blocking
| `adjust` | drift tolerances + corrective actions | advisory
| `dust`   | rollback / recovery / audit-trail preservation | advisory
| `intend` | north-star commitments + horizon aspirations | reporting
|===

Component health uses **Component Readiness Grades (CRG)**, an evidence-required
scale
(https://github.com/hyperpolymath/gossamer/blob/main/docs/governance/CRG-CRITERIA.adoc[criteria]).
Gossamer is graded **D** (Partial / alpha), targeting **C** (Self-Validated /
dogfooded); the D→C path is recorded in the
https://github.com/hyperpolymath/gossamer/blob/main/docs/governance/CRG-AUDIT-2026-04-18.adoc[2026-04-18 audit]
and STATE.a2ml.

== Compliance & security posture

* **Threat model** — STRIDE analysis, quarterly review:
https://github.com/hyperpolymath/gossamer/blob/main/docs/architecture/THREAT-MODEL.adoc[THREAT-MODEL.adoc].
* **Security policy** — coordinated disclosure via `SECURITY.md` (RSR file).
* **Supply chain** — OpenSSF Scorecard wired (`scorecard.yml` +
`scorecard-enforcer.yml`); OpenSSF Best Practices enrollment initiated. Actions
SHA-pinned; lockfiles committed; TruffleHog + secret-scanner, CodeQL, and SLSA
provenance + SBOM on releases.
* **Machine-readable state** — ecosystem position and session state in
https://github.com/hyperpolymath/gossamer/blob/main/.machine_readable/descriptiles/ECOSYSTEM.a2ml[ECOSYSTEM.a2ml]
and STATE.a2ml, updated each session.

== Risk register

Likelihood and impact are Low / Medium / High. Mitigations are those already in
place, not planned.

[cols="4,1,1,6",options="header"]
|===
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation (in place)
| **Formal-methods maturity** — alpha (~92% MVP); the proof surface could lag
the shipped ABI or harbour unproven obligations.
| Medium | High
| 15 ABI proof modules build green across two de-conflated packages
(`gossamer-abi.ipkg` + `gossamer-groove.ipkg`, one-way dependency);
`idris2 0.8.0 --typecheck`, `%default total`, **zero `believe_me`, zero axioms**.
| **Single-maintainer / bus-factor** — one Lead Maintainer owns core review and
release.
| High | Medium
| Machine-readable state + RSR structure keep the repo self-describing and
resumable; contractile gates encode policy for handover; CRG audits keep the true state visible.
| **Toolchain dependency** — pre-1.0 Ephapax and Idris2 0.8.0; upstream churn
could break builds or proofs.
| Medium | High
| Toolchains pinned (`idris2 0.8.0`, `zig 0.15.2`), verified locally and
re-typechecked in CI; the ABI is decoupled via the stable C symbol surface.
| **Platform-webview API drift** — WebKitGTK / WKWebView / WebView2 / Android JNI
evolve independently and can break native glue.
| Medium | Medium
| Per-platform Zig FFI behind one C ABI, dispatch routed by target; 192
integration tests plus Zig and Deno suites exercise the handler paths.
| **Supply-chain / ABI drift** — a hand-edited binding could diverge from native
exports, or a poisoned dependency/action could enter the build.
| Medium | High
| https://github.com/hyperpolymath/gossamer/blob/main/src/interface/abi/ForeignGen.idr[`Gossamer.ABI.ForeignGen`]
is *generated* (`just abi-gen`) to mirror every one of the ~135 `gossamer_*` exports, and CI enforces its freshness so the typechecked ABI cannot drift from the Zig FFI; actions are SHA-pinned and lockfiles + secret-scanning cover the rest.
| **Consumer-migration dependency** — headline adoption (IDApTIK, neurophone) is
in progress, so field-proven evidence is still thin.
| Medium | Medium
| Dogfooding tracked with grep-checkable evidence in STATE.a2ml; CRG held honestly at D until external targets confirm.
|===
